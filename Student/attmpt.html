Quiz Instructions for Students

Please read carefully before starting your attempt.

  • The quiz must be completed in a single attempt; attempt carefully as only one attempt is allowed.
  • Once the timer starts, pause or restart will not be available.
  • When the time ends, the quiz will be automatically submitted.
  • You may skip any question and attempt it later.
  • Auto-submit on focus change: If the quiz window is minimized or you switch tabs/windows, your quiz may be auto-submitted.
  • After the final submission, no reattempts will be allowed.
  • Use of mobile phones or external assistance is strictly prohibited.
  • Results will be shown either immediately after submission or later by the instructor, depending on the system policy.
  • Randomization: Questions and options will appear in a different order for each student.

Note: Keep the quiz window in focus. Switching apps or tabs can trigger automatic submission depending on institutional policy.

Academic Integrity: Any form of cheating or collaboration may lead to disqualification and disciplinary action.
